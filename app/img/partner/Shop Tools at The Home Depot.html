Tools That You Can Rely On

Think of us as your one-stop tool shop. As the #1 tool & hardware retailer, nobody has more power tools & accessories. From cordless drills and saws to grinders and power tool combo kits—you’ll find everything you need. 
 
Your next home improvement project deserves tools that are built to last. Make sure your toolbox is complete by visiting the tools store you can trust. Whether you’re an experienced professional in need of a new sander, or a DIYer picking up your first power tool, we’ve got you covered. We are proud to be your #1 destination for tools to get the project done. 
 
Tools for All Experience Levels 

For professionals, our tools shop offers an expanded assortment of tools with thousands of products available, including hard-to-find commercial items that are not available in store. If you’re working on a specific project and don’t own the tools you need, our tool rental service is exactly what you’re looking for. We offer a large assortment of reliable pro-grade and brand name tools for rent at more than 1,000 locations for your convenience. 

Not sure whether you need a table saw, band saw, or miter saw? Not to worry, our knowledgeable Store Associates are eager to help you find exactly what you need. Just lookout for that orange apron and help is on the way. When you’re at our hardware tools store, you can be sure that you’ll have assistance when you need it. 
 
Tools of All Kinds

In addition to everyday tools such as drills and wrenches, we also carry products for more advanced users, including air compressors and welders. From tool storage and automotive tools to woodworking tools and vacuums, we have everything you need to complete any project. Our home improvement tools will make your renovations easy and our everyday hardware tools make even the most daunting tasks a breeze. 
 
Tool Maintenance

We understand that even the best tools require maintenance every now and then, which is why we’re proud to offer our tool repair service. Our expert technicians can repair any brand of tools purchased from any store. So visit us today and prepare for your next project with our wide selection of hand tools from leading brands.
